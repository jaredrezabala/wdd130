Overview

Purpose

Dry Oar strives to give people an outstanding and safe experience with wild nature. We help you navigate rough waters by supplying you with the equipment and/or the guides you will need to safely head down the river. We know that family time is precious and we feel honored when families decide to visit us, that’s why we strive to give them the best we have. With our experienced guides, we assure you the best entertainment and also a different and fun way of learning about nature and its importance of it in our days. Rafting is an adventure sport and there is a lot to think about before your first trip, but don’t worry, we will guide you through this adventure, we understand that rafting can seem to be a scary outdoor activity but personally we think that anyone with the desire to have a good time can join this adventure, that’s why we strive to teach our visitors about the safety guidelines of the rides, the proper use of the gears and also what to do when they experience fear during the ride. We know that everyone has an adventurous spirit wanting to come out and Dry oar is the place for it to do it.

Audience

Any extreme sports lover and outgoing family would enjoy this experience. We are aware that we all have responsibilities to fulfill in our daily lives and more often than not we don’t set aside time for ourselves and our families because we know how demanding studies, work, and other tasks can be, we have designed flexible schedules to give you that time that you yearn for, we know you want to have fun and relax and that’s why different types of rides have been implemented, from a fun ride through the brave whitewater to a smooth and peaceful ride through the river. What about people or families who are not into extreme sports? Rafting is for everyone and we understand that can be daunting to face a new adventure that’s why we provide online tours to guide you through this wonderful experience without taking you from the comfort of your home without feeling ready for this experience. What about third-age and handicapped people? We share experiences with everyone, we have capable staff and professional guides that will take good care of you besides counting on special gear and safety protocols for you. You can access all this information from your desktop, laptop, or mobile device wherever you may be, just visit our website to find out more about how to get started.
